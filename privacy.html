Privacy Policy

CoinWatch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and rewards platform.

By using CoinWatch, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Usage data: Ads watched, coins earned, redemption history, login activity
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, IP address (collected automatically)
  • Payment details: UPI ID or crypto wallet address (only when you request a redemption)

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your CoinWatch account
  • To track your coin earnings and redemptions
  • To send you OTP verification emails and account notifications
  • To process your reward redemption requests
  • To prevent fraud and abuse of our platform
  • To improve our services and user experience

Advertising

CoinWatch uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

We also show rewarded video ads through third-party ad networks. These networks may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We participate in survey programs including CPX Research. Survey providers may collect demographic information in accordance with their privacy policies.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences (such as dark mode)
  • Analyse site traffic and usage patterns
  • Serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which is GDPR-compliant. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication.

Third-Party Services

CoinWatch uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, analytics
  • Google AdSense — Display advertising
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — Transactional email
  • CPX Research — Survey monetisation
  • Cloudflare — Security and performance

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing their data practices.
